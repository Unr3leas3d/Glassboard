# Glasboard: The Invisible Communication Layer for Live Presentations

**Target Audience:** Business Systems Managers, Team Leads, and Junior Presenters
**Estimated Duration:** 5 Minutes

---

## 1. The Challenge (1 Minute)
* **The Scenario:** Your junior staff member is driving a critical live presentation or software demo with clients or stakeholders. 
* **The Problem:** They get stuck, miss a key talking point, or hover over the wrong metric. As a manager, you are completely cut off. 
* **Current Workarounds Fail:** 
  * You can't speak up without interrupting the flow and undermining their authority.
  * Pinging them on Slack/Teams requires them to look away from the presentation, breaking eye contact and focus. 
  * "Next slide please" or "Click that button on the left" sounds unprofessional to the client.

## 2. The Solution: Glasboard's Private Overlay (1.5 Minutes)
* **What is Glasboard?** It is a real-time, transparent communication layer designed specifically for live presentations. 
* **How it Works for the Presenter:** Glasboard creates a transparent, click-through overlay window that sits on top of their screen. 
* **The Magic ("The Invisible Layer"):** Built using native OS-level protections (specifically the `contentProtected: true` architecture), **the Glasboard overlay is completely invisible to screen-sharing software** (like Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet). 
* **The Result:** The recipients on the call see a clean, professional presentation. The junior staff member sees your real-time guidance seamlessly overlaid on their screen.

## 3. Key Features in Action (1.5 Minutes)
* **The WhisperFlow Interface:** The presenter is never distracted by clunky toolbars. The UI is stripped down to a minimal, unobtrusive pill-bar at the bottom of the screen.
* **Laser Pointer Mode:** As the manager, you can press `Cmd+Shift+G` (or use the pill-bar) to instantly activate a red laser pointer on their screen. You can literally point to the exact button they need to click or the chart they should be referencing, without saying a word.
* **Real-time Annotations:** Powered by low-latency Excalidraw integration and Supabase real-time channels, you can draw arrows or circle key information. As soon as the point is made, the annotations can be cleared. 
* **Multi-Window Architecture:** The management dashboard (where you control settings and join the session) is completely separated from the transparent overlay, ensuring no administrative UI ever accidentally bleeds onto the screen.

## 4. The Business Impact & Conclusion (1 Minute)
* **Empowered Junior Staff:** They present with confidence, knowing their manager is right there as a "co-pilot" to guide them silently if needed.
* **Flawless Execution:** Stakeholders receive a smooth, uninterrupted presentation, boosting the perceived competence of the entire team.
* **Summary:** Glasboard transforms the isolating experience of screen sharing into a collaborative, guided environment—all behind the scenes.
